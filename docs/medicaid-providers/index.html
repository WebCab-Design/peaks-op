Medicaid Providers Colorado

Peaks Recovery Services is NOT not a Medicaid provider. That being said, we believe in getting people to the appropriate level of care and have provided a list of Medicaid/ free providers below within the state of Colorado. All information presented is to the best of our knowledge. There may be further resources that we are not aware of at this time.

Medicaid Providers Colorado

  • RESADA House - (719) 456-2600. Las Animas, Colorado. Medical Detox/ inpatient services.
  • Lutheran West Pines - (303) 467-4080 Denver, Colorado. Medical Detox Services ONLY.
  • Denver Cares Inpatient - (303) 436-3572 Denver, Colorado. Social Detox/ extended IOP services.
  • Mile High Behavioral Health - (303) 353-1469 Denver, Colorado. IOP/ OP services.
  • Arapahoe House - (303) 657-3700 Denver, Colorado. IOP/ OP services
  • Crossroads Turning Point - (719) 419-7959 Pueblo, Colorado. IOP/ OP services
  • Comprehensive Behavioral Health Center - (720) 419-7959 Denver, Colorado. IOP/ OP services
  • CoreVision - (719) 634-7161 Colorado Springs, Colorado. IOP/ OP services

Free Treatment Providers - Colorado

  • Stout Street Foundation - (866) 722-7040. Commerce City, Colorado. Inpatient, extended care IOP/ OP services. Therapeutic Community (TC) based behavioral modification program for long-term addicts.
  • Salvation Army - (303) 294-0827 Denver, Colorado. IOP/ OP services. Resident ial Living. Male ONLY facility.

A Caring Counseling Solution

Call Now: (888) 506-9818

The process for shutting off our cravings for drugs and alcohol, including the process for turning on important coping mechanisms to alleviate on going mental health issues, is not a light switch. Most people abusing drugs and alcohol, those neglecting their mental health, or both, have invested themselves in those behaviors for months, years, and even decades. At Peaks Recovery Services we believe that changes in behavior and lifestyle requires a significant investment to heal both our mind and body from our unhealthy past.
