Most people abusing drugs and alcohol, those neglecting their mental health, or both, have invested themselves in those behaviors for months, years, and even decades. At Peaks Recovery Services we believe that changes in behavior and lifestyle requires a significant investment to heal both our mind and body from our unhealthy past.
