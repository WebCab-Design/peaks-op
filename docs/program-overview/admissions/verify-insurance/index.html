Verify your insurance below

Peaks Recover Services are drug and alcohol addiction treatment providers in Colorado and work with most PPO insurance plans that have out-of-network benefits. Please submit your insurance information below and a representative from our company will contact you within 24-hours regarding your insurance benefits. If you are interested in cash payment options, please contact our admission line directly at (888) 506-9818.

For United Health Care/ Optum insurance policies, the client must call their insurer first and authorize Peaks Recovery Services to verify your benefits. If a reference number is given, please place that number in the comments section.
