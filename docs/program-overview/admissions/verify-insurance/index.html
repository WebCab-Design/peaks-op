Peaks Recover Servics are out-of-network providers and work with most insurance PPO plans. Please submit your insurance information below and a representative from our company will contact you within 24-hours regarding your insurance benefits. If you are interested in cash payment options, please contact our admission line directly at (888) 596-9818.

For United Health Care/ Optum policies, the client must call the insurer first and get a reference ID number. Please note that the policy holder cannot do this for the potential client, the actual client seeking treatment needs to call insurer to get the reference number. Please place reference number in the comments section.

Peaks Services

Peaks Recovery Services is a community based IOP program serving the greater Colorado Springs area. Our program specifically works with both young adults (18-35) and working professionals who struggle with substance use disorder and process addictions. We also incorporate an integrative dual diagnosis approach as part of treatment.
