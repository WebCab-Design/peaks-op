Admission Process

Providing you with clinical services that match your needs is important to us. We would be happy to work with you to see if our treatment setting and your needs are a match. Admission into Peaks Services is a straightforward and easy process:

Option 1: Call us to discuss your situation at (888) 506-9818

Option 2: If you have insurance and would like us to check how your insurance would work with our program, click on “Verify Insurance”. After submitting your insurance information, we will be back in touch with you within 24 hours.

If we discover, together, that Peaks Services is the right fit for you, we will walk you through the admission process. This will include verifying your insurance and setting up an admission date and time.
